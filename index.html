About me

I'm a front-end developer based in Rio de Janeiro and passionate about technology, Design and Motion.
Graduated as a Control and Automation Engineer and on a carrer transition, I had my first contact with the programming world when I was still in university, where I was able to discover and develop my programming logic and code in languages related to Control and Automation. However, it was after the university that I discovered the web development and since I've always been interested in Design and creating things from scratch, I fell in love with front-end.
Currently I'm studying and improving my HTML, CSS, SASS, JavaScript and React JS and i'm also familiar with UI Design and Figma.
